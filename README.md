## Why OctoHook?

- **One binary** ‚Äî No Node, no Python, just works
- **Smart caching** ‚Äî Skip hooks when nothing changed
- **Any project** ‚Äî Go, Node, Python, Rust, whatever
- **Beautiful TUI** ‚Äî See your hooks at a glance
